FAQ

1- How i could get my profits ?
A: our website and yours are separate, your customers add funds on your website and buy with your price and on the other hand you add funds with us and buy with our price AUTOMATICALLY.

2- How i could enable VISA/MasterCard Payments?
A: MOST of Visa and MasterCard website providers are already programmed and ready to use BUT you need to have an Working/Fully Verified Account with them then you can just move your api to it.
NOTE: we are not related with those websites and we can’t open an account for you with them.

3- Can i choose my website currency ?
A: YES you can BUT when you choose any other currency except USD then you will not get all payment method to link because the default and most using currency is USD.
NOTE: if you want to change your website currency please open a ticket and we will change it for you.

4- Is the 25 USD per month includes the domain ?
A: NO, you MUST purchase the domain by your own.
NOTE: we just need a domain , everything else from HOSTING , SSL … is from us.

5- Can i change my domain after i order the website ?
A: YES you can , please open a ticket for that.

6- Can i have any discounts ?
A: Unfortunately we don’t provide any discounts for child panels for now as the discounts are related to Account Status for now.

7- What if I want to stop using the child panel ?
A: you can stop anytime as we don’t do any auto payments on child panels so if you want to renew your website you have to do it manually from child panel page.

8- Can I have the same design of AmazingSMM website ?
A: NO you can’t and you need to do your own design.

9- What will happen if i did not pay the monthly fees and my website expired?
A: it will be frozen automatically and neither you or your clients could access to it until you renew it again.
NOTE: we always send the renewing reminder via email before 7 days of the expiry.

10- do you have annual or lifetime subscription ?
A: unfortunately not for now.
